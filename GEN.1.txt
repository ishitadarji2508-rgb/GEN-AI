What is  GEN AI?


1.write a short paragraph in your own words explaining what Generative AI(GEN AI) is , and  give two real world examples  of  GenAI application you have seen or used. 
Ans :  Generative AI (GenAI) is a branch of artificial intelligence that can create new content such as text, images, videos, music, and code. It learns patterns from large datasets and uses that knowledge to generate original outputs based on user instructions or prompts. Unlike traditional AI, which mainly analyzes existing data, GenAI can produce new and creative content.

1. ChatGPT – I have used ChatGPT to answer questions, generate content, and help with assignments.
2. DALL-E – DALL-E can create unique images from text descriptions provided by users.

 Comparison of Generative AI (GenAI) and Traditional AI
Aspect
	Generative AI (GenAI)
	Traditional AI
	Data Input
	Learns from large datasets and user prompts to create new content.
	Uses structured data and predefined rules to analyze or predict outcomes.
	Creativity
	Can generate original and creative content such as text, images, music, and videos.
	Focuses on recognizing patterns and making decisions; does not create original content.
	Output Format
	Produces new content that may be different each time.
	Produces fixed outputs such as classifications, predictions, or recommendations.
	Purpose
	Content creation and generation.
	Data analysis, prediction, and automation.
	Examples
	ChatGPT, DALL-E, Gemini.
	Spam filters, face recognition systems, fraud detection systems.
	Conclusion:
Generative AI is designed to create new and original content, while traditional AI is mainly used to analyze data, identify patterns, and make predictions or decisions based on existing information.
